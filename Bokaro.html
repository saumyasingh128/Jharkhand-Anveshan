THE STRUCTURE

Bokaro is one of India's first global active planned cities. Famous for the Bokaro Steel Plant (BSL), it is a city of wide avenues, sectors, and lush greenery.

SAIL & The Township

The Bokaro Steel Plant (BSL) is one of the largest in Asia. It was built with Soviet collaboration, which is reflected in the city's architecture and planning.

The city is divided into 12 Sectors. It is also known as the **"Education Hub of Jharkhand"**, producing countless IITians and civil servants every year from its prestigious schools like DPS and Chinmaya.

Industry

Home to SAIL, Vedanta Electrosteel, and BIADA industrial area.

Education

Known for its high density of top-tier schools and coaching centers.

LEISURE ZONES

Despite the heavy industry, Bokaro is incredibly green. Explore its lakes and parks.

City Park

The heart of the city. A massive park featuring a large artificial lake with boating facilities, a toy train, and a Japanese garden vibe. It hosts the famous Chhath Puja.

Jagannath Temple

A stunning white replica of the Puri Jagannath Temple located in Sector 4. The Rath Yatra here is a grand spectacle.

Garga Dam

Located about 12 km from the city center, this dam on the Garga River is a lush green picnic spot and the primary water source for the township.

SECTOR FLAVORS

From the famous Litti Chokha to cosmopolitan treats, Bokaro's food scene is vibrant.

Litti Chokha

The soul food of the region. The stalls near Bokaro Mall and City Centre serve it hot with spicy chutney.

South Indian Fare

Thanks to the diverse PSU population, Ram Mandir market and Sector 4 offer authentic Dosa and Idli.

Chowmein & Rolls

The evening street food scene in Sector 4 is dominated by spicy Indo-Chinese Chowmein and Egg Rolls.
