About The Tour

Spiritual Core

Jharkhand is a significant spiritual center, prominently featuring sites sacred to both Hinduism and Jainism. It is the home of one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, attracting millions of devotees during the annual Shravan Mela pilgrimage. The state also features other ancient and unique temples like the Sun Temple in Ranchi, built in the shape of a chariot.

The Land of Forests and Waterfalls

Jharkhand, famously known as the "Land of Forests," is celebrated for its numerous and beautiful waterfalls, particularly those clustered around the capital, Ranchi, which is often nicknamed the "City of Waterfalls." The landscape offers diverse **ecosystems** and **adventure sports** opportunities.
