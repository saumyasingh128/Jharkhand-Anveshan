About Dassam Falls

Dassam Falls, also known as Dassam Ghagh, is a spectacular natural waterfall located near Taimara village in Jharkhand's Ranchi district.

Formed by the Kanchi River, it drops 44 meters (144 feet) into a deep plunge pool. The name "Dassam" means "ten" in Mundari language, representing the ten streams created during monsoon.

Surrounded by lush forests, it's an ecological haven with rich biodiversity, attracting nature enthusiasts and photographers year-round.

Important Safety Notice

Swimming is strictly prohibited here. The undercurrents are strong and unpredictable, and the rocks are sharp and slippery.

Several accidents have occurred due to disregard of safety rules. Please admire the beauty from a safe distance and follow all posted guidelines.
