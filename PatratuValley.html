Valley & Water

Patratu Valley is a unique destination where you descend from lush green hills directly into a massive blue reservoir. The dam, built on the Nalkari River, supplies water to the Patratu Thermal Power Station and creates a breathtaking landscape.

Patratu Dam

A massive reservoir supplying water to the thermal power station and creating a stunning aquatic playground for visitors.

Height: 45.7 meters | Length: 365 meters

Visitor Information

Best Time to Visit

Monsoon (July - September)

Lush green landscapes, flowing waterfalls, and misty roads create magical views.

Winter (October - February)

Clear skies, pleasant weather, and perfect conditions for photography and boating.

Avoid Summer (March - June)

Hot and humid weather, water levels may be lower, less comfortable for outdoor activities.

How to Reach

From Ranchi

30 km via NH43 (approx 1 hour drive). Well-marked road with signs.

Public Transport

Regular buses from Ranchi Bus Stand to Patratu. Taxis available at ₹800-1000 for round trip.

Entry Timings & Fees

Open daily 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Entry fee: ₹20 per person, ₹50 for vehicles.

Travel Tips

Fuel Up

Last petrol pump is in Ranchi. Ensure sufficient fuel.

Camera Ready

Carry your camera for stunning panoramic shots.

Carry Snacks

Limited food options available at the dam site.
