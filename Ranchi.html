Iconic Landmarks

From spiritual heights to natural wonders, explore the must-visit places

Hundru Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand at 320ft, located on the Subarnarekha River. A spectacular sight during monsoon.

Best Time: Monsoon

Jagannath Temple

17th-century architectural marvel, replica of Puri Jagannath Temple. Famous for its massive Rath Yatra attracting lakhs of devotees.

Spiritual Site

Rock Garden

Man-made wonder featuring sculptures, waterfalls, and lush gardens carved out of natural rocks. Perfect family picnic spot.
